Route95 is an Audio/Art experience where players make their own music with a set of instruments provided for them. They will have options to modify the instrument sounds using the built in sound effects and once they're done making their song, players will then be putting them in a playlist to play in live mode



This is where we start driving through Route95 and enjoy the playlist we created. The environment is procedurally generated generated using the songs frequencies. players can also pick what instruments to play picking the numbers which corresponds to an instrument and using the keyboard as the instrument
