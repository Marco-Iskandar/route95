Welcome to Route 95

"Rock the desert"

Our Game

Route 95 is an Audio/Art experience where players make their own music with a set of instruments provided for them. They will have the option to modify the instrument sounds using the built in sound effects and once they're done making their song, players will then place them in a playlist to play in live mode and experience the world.

The environment is procedurally generated generated using the songs frequencies. Players can also choose which instruments to play by selecting the numbers which corresponds to an instrument and using the keyboard to play notes.

Our Team Members

My name is Aaron Desin, and I'm the lead developer and artist on Route 95. I'm responsible for making sure the development process goes smoothly, in addition to contributing code and art assets for the project. Even though I grew up in northern California, I've always had a fascination with the Palm Desert rock scene (particularly Queens of the Stone Age), and the capstone senior game design project sequence at University of California, Santa Cruz offered the perfect opportunity to express that passion, in the form of Route 95. My other interests include composing music, eating, and extreme ironing.
